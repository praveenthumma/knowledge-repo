##  What is Software Architecture?
	The definition has 4 dimensions
		 1.Architectural Style as Starting point
		  2.Architecture Characteristics it must support
		  3.Logical components to implement its behaviour
		  4.Architecture decisions to justify it all
#### Architectural Characteristics
Define capabilities of a system
- Availability
- Reliability
- Testability
- Scalability
- Security
- Agility
- Fault Tolerance
- Elasticity
- Recoverability
- Performance
- Deployability
- Learnability

#### **Expectations from an architect**
 - Make architectural decisions
 - Continually analyze architecture
 - Keep current with latest trends
 - Ensure compliance with decision
 - Understand Diverse technologies , frameworks , platforms and environments
 - Know the business Domain
 - Lead a team and possess inter personal skills
 - Understand and navigate organisational politics.
